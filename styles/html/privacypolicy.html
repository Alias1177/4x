We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Prevent Fraud: To detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized transactions, and other illegal activities.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with the following entities in certain situations:
