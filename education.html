What is Copy Trading?

Copy trading allows you to automatically copy the trades of experienced traders. This allows you to benefit from the expertise of professionals without needing to make the trades yourself. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced investor, copy trading can help you grow your portfolio in a low-effort way.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Copy Trading?

Copy trading allows you to automatically copy the trades of professional traders. It’s a great way to diversify your portfolio and learn from experienced individuals.

How do I select a trader to copy?

You can browse trader profiles based on performance, risk level, and strategy. Make sure to choose traders that align with your investment goals and risk tolerance.

Can I stop copying a trader at any time?

Yes, you can stop copying a trader at any time, and your account will stop automatically mirroring their trades.

How do I withdraw my profits?

You can withdraw your profits by going to your account settings and selecting the withdrawal option. Make sure to follow the withdrawal process outlined on the platform.
